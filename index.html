The Offside Rule

When your teammate kicks the ball, no part of you that can score — your head, shoulder, knee or foot — can be past the last defender.

Move Isla with the arrow keys. Hit PASS when she's behind (or level with) the line. Level is fine! Arms and hands don't count. After each pass the VAR cam shows exactly which body part was over.
